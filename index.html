🔐 How It Works
1
File Upload
You drag & drop or select any file up to 50MB. ContentShield accepts images, videos, audio files, and PDFs.
2
SHA-256 Hashing
Our backend reads the raw bytes and runs them through SHA-256, producing a unique 64-character hex digest in milliseconds.
3
Nibble-Level Similarity
Beyond exact matches, we compare nibble patterns to catch near-duplicates — even re-encoded or slightly edited versions.
4
Stored in Vault
The fingerprint is saved alongside your file metadata. Every future upload is instantly compared against your full vault.

💡 Why SHA-256?
SHA-256 is deterministic — the same file always produces the same hash. It's collision-resistant, meaning two different files will never produce the same fingerprint. Used by Bitcoin, SSL certificates, and major cloud providers.
